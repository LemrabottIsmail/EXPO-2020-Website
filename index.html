🌟 Brief Description 🌟

What is an Expo? 🌎

🌎 World Expos are a universal gathering of countries from all over the world devoted to discovering solutions for our daily life challenges through a worldwide theme. World Expos have offered a platform for displaying the best and most efficient innovations which aided in improving and developing today’s world. In addition, the World Expo allowed people from all over the world to present their culture and share important information about their countries and hometowns.

Expo2020 Dubai 🇦 🇪

The World Expo was held in the United Arab Emirates 🇦 🇪 (Dubai) from 1 October 2021 to 31 March 2022. The World Expo took place in Dubai as it is considered one of the world’s most popular destinations to visit. In fact, Dubai has been ranked ahead of international cities like Rome, Paris, and London indicating that Dubai is the world’s most popular tourist destination.

The Purpose Of Expo2020 Dubai 🎯

The purpose of Expo2020 is to bring the world together in one place to exchange perspectives and inspire action to deliver efficient solutions to worldwide challenges. In addition, people will have the opportunity to have a glimpse of the world without actually traveling. Thus, people will learn about different traditions and cultures by demonstrating how people live in each society. 🎯

🎯 Expo2020 Dubai could be described as an exhibition of the entire world. 191 countries will participate in hosting povilions, showcasing their culture, cuisine, art, technology, innovations, and inventions. Morover, Expo2020 Dubai will allow people to experience walking around the globe from one city ❕
